Basic DEI Principles for a more inclusive educational space

  • Diversify your curriculum: Include texts, histories, and examples from a wide range of voices and communities, and assess whose perspectives are represented in your materials.
  • Use inclusive teaching strategies: Incorporate a variety of teaching methods, assessment types, and modalities (e.g., group work, presentations, anonymous submissions).
  • Reflect on and address bias: Regularly examine your teaching practices, language, and assumptions for inclusivity and fairness.
  • Model inclusive behaviour: Demonstrate empathy, promote respectful classroom dialogue, and respond constructively to incidents of bias.
  • Build strong relationships: Get to know your students and their backgrounds to foster a sense of belonging in the classroom.
